History



Six Degrees is launched in 1997

Created by Andrew Weinreich, Six Degrees was the first social networking site, allowing users to create profiles, have a friends list, and contact each other through private messaging.

MySpace (and Friendster) were launched in 2003

MySpace has profile customization, instant messaging, blogging and forum abilities, bulletins, and ways to promote music.

Facebook was launched in 2004, quickly gaining popularity.

Facebook includes a friends list, a post timeline, video sharing, a news feed, pinned posts, notifications, and an inbox.

Twitter was launched in 2006

Twitter included circles, pinned tweets, retweets, analytics, direct messages, hashtags, and display names.

Instagram (and Snapchat) were launched in 2010

Instagram includes photo sharing, reels (short videos), creating posts, sharing stories, shoppable posts, comment pins, and the ability to view liked posts.

TikTok was launched in 2016

TikTok includes video recording, video editing, video sharing, filters/effects, sounds/voice-overs, live streaming, and playlists.
